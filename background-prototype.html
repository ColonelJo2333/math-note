Scribbles on Manifolds

This is a prototype layout displaying the mathematical pencil sketches in the background. The core requirement is that these sketches remain out of the way of the primary text, ensuring perfect readability.

String Worldsheet (Pants Diagram)

In the top-left corner, you should see a subtle drawing. It outlines a closed string splitting into two representing string interactions, connected smoothly like an inverted pair of pants. Small dashed segments represent intermediate states.

Poincaré Disk (AdS Space)

In the bottom-right corner, we sweep orthogonal arcs mimicking geodesics to infinity. By modifying the roughness and opacity, multiple overlaps simulate layered graphite. Look closely at the asymptotes near the right and bottom edges.
